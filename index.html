Common Myths About Menstruation

Myth: Periods are dirty.

Fact: Periods are a natural and clean biological process.

Myth: You shouldn't exercise on your period.

Fact: Exercise can help reduce cramps and boost mood.

Myth: You can’t get pregnant during your period.

Fact: It is possible, though less likely.
