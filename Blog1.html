Nutrition for Healthier Periods

Leafy Greens

Leafy Green Vegetables

Spinach, kale, and amaranth are rich in iron and magnesium, which help replenish blood loss and reduce fatigue.

Bananas & Citrus

Fruits (Bananas & Citrus)

High in fiber, potassium, and vitamin C, fruits like bananas, oranges, and papayas reduce bloating and improve mood.

Omega-3 Foods

Omega-3 Rich Foods

Salmon, flaxseeds, and walnuts reduce inflammation, ease cramps, and stabilize mood swings.

Dark Chocolate

Dark Chocolate

70% cocoa dark chocolate contains magnesium and antioxidants to relax muscles and reduce cravings.

Nuts & Seeds

Nuts and Seeds

Almonds, sunflower seeds, and pumpkin seeds are packed with healthy fats, zinc, and vitamin E to balance hormones.

Whole Grains

Whole Grains

Brown rice, oats, and millets provide complex carbs and B-vitamins that improve energy and mood stability.

Herbal Teas

Herbal Teas

Chamomile, ginger, and peppermint tea reduce bloating, cramps, and help with sleep quality.

Iron-Rich Foods

Iron-Rich Foods

Lentils, tofu, jaggery, and raisins are great to replenish iron lost during periods and prevent fatigue.
